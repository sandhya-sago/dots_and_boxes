Dots and Boxes game

Starting with an empty grid of dots, two players take turns adding a single horizontal or vertical line between two unjoined adjacent dots. The player who completes the fourth side of a 1Ã—1 box earns one point and takes another turn. (A point is recorded by placing a "A" or "B" identifying the player in the box) The game ends when no more lines can be placed. The winner is the player with the most points. This game uses 5X5 dots.
